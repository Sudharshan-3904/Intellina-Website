TechTrivia

General Rules

  • The quiz will be conducted in two rounds - a written round and a buzzer round
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding
  • Any appeals or disputes must be raised in a peaceful and calm manner

Round 1 (Written Round)

  • The written round will consist of a specified number of questions
  • The questions will cover a wide range of topics related to AI and will be designed to test the participant's knowledge and understanding
  • The participants will be given a fixed amount of time to complete the written round(30 mins)
  • The answer sheets will be evaluated by the judges, and the top-scoring participants will qualify for Round 2

Round 2 (Buzzer Round)

  • The buzzer round will be a rapid-fire format, where questions will be asked, and participants must buzz in to answer
  • Unlimited questions will be asked within a time frame of 30 minutes
  • Each correct answer will earn points for the team, and incorrect answers may result in negative points or passing the question to the next team
  • The team with the maximum points at the end of the 30 minutes will be declared the winner

Tie-Breaker Round

  • In case of a tie in Round 2, a tie-breaker round will be organized
  • The tie-breaker round will consist of a set of questions, and the team that answers the most questions correctly will be declared the winner.

General Conduct

  • Participants are expected to maintain decorum and respect the rules and regulations of the quiz
  • Any form of cheating, misbehavior, or disruptive conduct will lead to disqualification
  • The organizers reserve the right to modify or amend the rules as deemed necessary for the smooth conduct of the quiz
