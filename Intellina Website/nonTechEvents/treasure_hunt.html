Treasure Hunt

Event Description

Venue: Library Conference Hall

Embark on an exhilarating journey of wit and exploration in our high school's thrilling Treasure Hunt event! Decode clues, unravel mysteries, and race against time to claim the ultimate prize. Are you ready to unleash your inner adventurer?

Event Rules

  • Participant Eligibility:
    • This event is open to all!
    • Participants must adhere to the code of conduct throughout the event.
  • Team Formation:
    • 3 - 5 Members per team
    • Each team must nominate a team leader responsible for communication and coordination.
  • Event Duration
    • The treasure hunt event will take place within a specified time frame, typically not exceeding 3 hours.
  • Safety Precaution
    • Participants must adhere to all safety guidelines and instructions provided by the event organizers.
    • Running in crowded or restricted areas is prohibited.

FAQ's

  • Are player ratings revealed during Bidding?

    No

  • Can unsold players be bought in the acceleration auction at the end?

    Yes

  • In case of tied ratings, who wins?

    The team with the highest purse is declared the winner.

  • Is retention of players allowed?

    No

  • How is the team name chosen?

    Team names are written on folded sheets, and the team leader has to pick randomly one sheet.

  • Does ODI and Test performance considered for ranking?

    No
