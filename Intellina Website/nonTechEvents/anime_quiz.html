Anime Quiz

Event Description

A quiz of three levels in which each team guesses the anime character or the scene correctly to earn a point. The team with the highest score at the end will win.

Venue: IT110

Event Rules

  • Team Size - 2 or 3 members
  • The first team to press the buzzer is given chance to guess the answer and given a point if the answer is correct.
  • If the answer is wrong, the chance is given to the team who pressed the buzzer next.
  • Each team must have a mobile or laptop to join the buzzer room and the whatsapp group.
